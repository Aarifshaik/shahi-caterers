Founder of Masora

Founder of Masora

With over 15 years of expertise in business management, hospitality, events, and marketing, Mohammad Shafeeq is the visionary behind Masora. His journey began at 19, and by 2017, he had expanded into venue bookings, destination weddings, and luxury catering—driven by a passion for curating world-class fine dining experiences. Recognized as the "Debutante Business of the Year" by Economic Times in 2018, he has managed bookings for over 2,500 venues and collaborated with esteemed luxury hotel chains, including Taj-IHCL, Hyatt, Marriott International, Hilton, Accor, ITC, and Leela Hotels.

Shafeeq's vision blends grassroots authenticity with premium cuisines, ensuring every plate delights with authentic flavors and meticulous attention to detail. His expertise in managing high-profile events ensures impeccable execution, elevating every celebration with timeless elegance. Committed to redefining hospitality, he continuously enhances and simplifies experiences for clients and partners alike. Beyond his professional pursuits, he is passionate about travel, connecting with inspiring individuals, and embracing fitness and adventure sports.
